Extreme Durability

SURGICAL EQUIPMENT

Precision Detail Instrumentation

Concours results demand absolute measurement. We employ high-end diagnostics to ensure paint correction is safe, uniform, and mathematically flawless.

METROLOGY & CALIBRATION

Surgical Paint Analysis

Digital Paint Depth Gauges

We measure clearcoat thickness down to 0.1 microns across multiple points. This guarantees we compound safely without compromising clearcoat structural integrity.

Dual-Action Random Orbital Polishers

Using gear-driven machines that rotate and oscillate simultaneously, we eliminate paint burning and compound haze, leaving a hologram-free finish.

Light Tunnel Inspection Grid

Our multi-temperature LED tunnel mimics direct sun, high overcast, and monochromatic spectra, revealing micro-marring invisible to standard workshop bulbs.

Reflection Glossmeters

Laser reflection meters calculate light scatter before and after compounding, verifying a mirror reflectivity score exceeding 95 Gloss Units (GU).
