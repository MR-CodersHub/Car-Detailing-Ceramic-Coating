ISO Cleanroom Specifications

True ceramic bonding cannot happen in a standard garage. Dust, humidity, and airflow are strictly regulated in our detailing chambers to ensure flawless coating adhesion.

99.995% Air Filtration

OUR SATISFACTION SEAL

The Triple-Inspection Standard

Before your car key is returned, the vehicle undergoes three independent validation checks to ensure absolute detailing perfection.

1. Thickness Calibration

Digital electromagnetic thickness checks ensure we compound paint panels safely without diminishing clearcoat depth profiles.

2. Reflection Gloss Scan

Precision laser reflection glossmeters audit paint reflection at multiple angles, verifying a specular gloss rating above 95 GU.

3. Master Detail Sign-Off

An IDA-certified Master Detailer executes a final visual checklist under specialized diagnostic lights to ensure zero defects remain.

4. Warranty Registry

Your unique coating batch number, execution timestamp, and master details are logged in our registry database, guaranteeing transferable coverage.
