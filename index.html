THE PATH TO PERFECTION

Our Detailing Process Timeline

We follow a strict, surgical five-stage process to guarantee absolute quality. Here is the path your vehicle undergoes to achieve the ultimate shine.

01

Decontamination Wash

We perform an intensive two-bucket foam bath, iron fallout decontamination, and clay bar treatment to clear all embedded microscopic road film.

02

Paint Analysis & Taping

Using digital electromagnetic paint depth meters, we calculate the clearcoat thickness before taping off sensitive trim, rubber seals, and plastics.

03

Multi-Stage Correction

Certified specialists perform machine compounding and fine polishing to permanently level scratch ridges, swirls, and color oxidation.

04

Ceramic Bonding

The panel surfaces are chemically wiped clean of polishing oils, followed by the meticulous hand application and curing of premium 9H quartz coatings.

05

Quality Inspection

Every square inch is cross-inspected using monochromatic inspection spotlights. The car is cured under dynamic infrared wave lamps before final handover.

FREQUENT QUESTIONS

Detailing FAQ

Find answers to the most common questions regarding paint protection coatings, PPF films, and proper vehicle upkeep.

A Ceramic Coating is a premium liquid nanopolymer that chemically bonds with your vehicle's factory clearcoat. It creates a rigid glass-like barrier that repels water, road chemicals, brake dust, acid rain, bird droppings, and UV radiation, while providing extreme hydrophobic surface sheeting and self-cleaning attributes.

Depending on the package selected, a premium paint correction and ceramic application takes between 2 to 4 working days. This timeframe allows us to complete decontamination, multi-stage machine leveling of scratches, precise ceramic application, and full infrared lamp heat curing.

While 9H quartz ceramic provides extreme protection against micro-swirls, spiderweb lines, and chemical etching, it does not prevent direct high-velocity rock chips or deep key scratches. For complete physical impact protection, we recommend combining Ceramic Coating with our Self-Healing Paint Protection Film (PPF).

You should wash your car using a pH-neutral car shampoo, the traditional two-bucket wash method with microfibre wash mitts, and dynamic blow-drying or soft drying towels. Avoid automatic touch-washes which utilize harsh alkaline detergents and abrasive brushes that scratch the glass film.
